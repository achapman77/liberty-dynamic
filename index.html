The Safer Smarter Flash-Bang
Superior Safety | Superior Function | Superior Efficiency

Existing Flash Bangs Are

Dangerous & Costly

Fragmentation

Existing flash-bang devices may initiate secondary fragmentation that may cause severe injury to nearby people.

Fire & Smoke

The explosive event uses flash-powder. The residual heat may ignite fires and spew smoke that reduces crucial visibility in critical situations.

Unreliable Fuse

Existing pyrotechnic delay fuses have a wide delay deviation. This forces operators to either wait longer than tactically advantageous to enter area or risk "eating the bang."

Lawsuits

An individual lawsuit as a result of an improperly deployed flash-bang causing injury or harm can easily exceed $1M.

The use of flash-bangs and their potential to harm is under increasing scrutiny from lawmakers and media.

Operating Costs

A single-use flash-bang's per unit cost makes them exceedingly expensive for effective training.

Additionally, the classification of flash-bangs as explosive devices, requires specialized and expensive shipping and storage solutions.

How It Works

The Safer smarter flash-bang

Superior Safety

Ports located at bottom of device direct a powerful external noise-flash event.

Since the noise-flash event occurs in free space well above the ground, this virtually eliminates the risk of secondary fragmentation from debris.

The binary energetic material contained within the device allows for drastically lowered volatility levels, creating a vastly safer and more reliable device.

The device has an ultra-low fire & smoke hazard due to the efficient burn of the energetic material and the device remains cool to the touch.

Any failure upon deployment and device renders safe in 60 seconds.

Superior Design

Deployment mechanics match existing flash-bang devices, therefore minimal training will be required.

A digital "smart" fuse improves operational control through increased adaptability and a zero-time delay deviation.

The smart fuse can be factory programmed to any delay length.

Emitting a powerful and disorienting report, the device’s energetic sound and light output surpasses industry standards. The output is also factory scalable to specification (Tested to 184db).

Semi-flat ergonomic design promotes improved target accuracy as the unit does not roll after deployment.

LED indicator light provides device condition feedback.

Superior Efficiency

Liberty Dynamic's Device & Training units are reloadable, thereby reducing overall expenditure costs.

Safe and inexpensive reloadable training cartridges enable personnel to be more adequately trained while still maximizing cost-effectiveness.

Most importantly, the low volatility energetic material and very low fragmentation potential should reduce costly lawsuits and insurance claims resulting from improperly deployed or flawed devices.

Liberty Dynamic is aggressively seeking a classification that will simplify shipping and storage requirements.

EASY ADOPTION & NEW OPPORTUNITIES

Enhanced Training

Minimal initial training required as the device has similar deployment mechanics to current flash-bangs.

Liberty Dynamic's trainer is designed to the same form factor as the original.

Economical reloadable training cartridges significantly reduce training costs and help improve operator proficiency.

Hand Deployment

Minimal training required as mechanical action is exactly the same as existing devices.

Smart Fuse Delay and Payloads can be customized to specification.

Uses include:

  • Perimeter Security
  • Crowd Control
  • Convoy, Equipment, & Aircraft Security
  • Active Shooter

sUAS DEPLOYMENT

Liberty Dynamic’s Smart Fuse Enhanced Diversion Device has been adapted to be deployed by a compact delivery system affixed to a sUAS (aerial drone).

The compact delivery system does not drop the device from the sUAS. Rather, the energetic event is propagated in a directional cone from the undercarriage.

Irritants, dye, and rubber BBs are optional payloads.

The compact delivery system is designed to adapt to currently fielded sUAS technology. There is no need for the customer to buy a new sUAS platform.
